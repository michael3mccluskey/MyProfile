Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT?

My primary interest in IT is its integration with business in order to increase the customer experience.
This is seen a lot in children’s toys these days, IT elements are added to toys to increase their staying power and overall marketability.
There are countless examples, but Lego has a few products that when scanned by a smart phone display an image or play a story,
if the app keeps adding more features children can keep benefiting from the same toy much longer and companies can gain by releasing more pieces to add to the collection.
With the popularity of videogames physical toys need to evolve and become more IT heavy like Pokémon cards having the ability to become virtual cards
or Amiibo statues becoming playable characters in games.

When did your interest in IT start?

I’ve always had an interest in IT, when I was younger my dad would collect old PC’s and break them down using the parts to make better ones.
My interest in IT was always there but it really picked up when I took year 10 IT.
This soon became my favourite class because all the lessons were so different and interesting one day you would be disassembling and
reassembling a PC the next you would be using that PC to make a video game. I enjoyed my time in year 10 so much that I completed a certificate III in Information Technology
in years 11 and 12. Looking back my IT class has some of my fondest memories of school.
